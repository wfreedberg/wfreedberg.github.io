Tide Pool Exploration

2–3 hours · Easy · Family friendly

Discover the small creatures of the low-tide flats. A hands-on outing perfect for curious kids and adults alike.

When the tide draws back it leaves behind a world of hidden life — crabs, snails, tiny fish, and more, tucked among the rocks and pools. We'll wade in gently, turn over a few stones, and get a close look at the animals that call this in-between zone home, always returning them carefully to where we found them.

Great for families; children are very welcome with a participating adult. Wear shoes that can get wet and bring a towel and a change of clothes for little ones.
